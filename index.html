CleanPay is a simple waste management system that ensures fairness in how people pay for garbage collection. After making one payment, you get 4 or more collection counts each month, depending on the company's agreement with you. You won’t need to pay again for 30 days unless you use up all your collection counts. Every time your garbage is collected, a count is removed from your card. Once all your counts are used, you’ll need to pay again. This system helps make sure everyone pays fairly and only for the services they use.
